About Psychologist Erika Studnek

I am a licensed psychologist in the United States, specializing in online psychotherapy and telehealth services. With years of experience, I provide professional support to individuals, couples, and families across the US and Mexico.

My approach is integrative, combining evidence-based techniques such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) with a compassionate, person-centered approach. I am committed to helping you achieve emotional well-being and personal growth.
